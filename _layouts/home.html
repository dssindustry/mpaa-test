BGA Style Guide

The purpose of this document is to provide information and guideance on various styling aspects to be used consistantly across business.gov.au and its related websites.

Scope

The following sites use all or parts of this style guide:

  • Business.gov.au
  • Business or hobby tool
  • Country of Origin Labelling (CoOL)
  • ABLIS
  • SOBRS
