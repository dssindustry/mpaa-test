What you need to know

  • If you’re not sure about the answers you’ve given, it’s a good idea to chat to your financial advisor or contact the Australian Taxation Office (ATO), who can take into account your complete situation.
  • We’ve covered the basic business and tax registrations. We’ll ask for more information about your business when you apply, which will help us work out if your business should consider other registrations.
  • Remember - registering your business name doesn't stop other businesses from using it for their products or services. To stop others from using your business name, you need to apply for a trade mark.
